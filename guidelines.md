Official University Website Development
Scope of Work & Project Phases

Client: IRAB
Service Provider: Giraso – Digital Solutions
Project Type: Official University Website & Digital Platform

1. Phase One – Official Website (Included in Current Pricing)

This phase focuses on building the official public-facing website of the university and an administration dashboard to manage content efficiently.
The website will be designed to be scalable, allowing future extensions without rebuilding the system.

Estimated Duration

⏱ 1 month from project start date.

A. Public Website (Official Site)

The public website will include the following sections:

Home Page

Hero section (main message / identity)

Announcements and publications

Partners section

Faculties overview

Administration / About Us

Organizational chart (organigramme)

University presentation (mission, vision, structure)

Services

Departments

Faculties

Clubs

Laboratories

Blog / Publications

Articles and official announcements

News and academic publications

Contact Page

Official contact information

Contact form

Social media links

B. Administration Dashboard

A secure admin dashboard will be provided to manage website content.

Dashboard Features:

Create, edit, and delete articles

Manage blog posts and publications

Upload and manage media (images, documents)

Add and manage departments and faculties

Manage social media links and phone numbers

C. Technical Setup (Included)

Website deployment

Hosting setup (1 year)

Domain name configuration (1 year)

2. Future Phases – Not Included in Current Pricing

The following features are not included in the current development cost and will be discussed, specified, and priced separately in future agreements.

A. Mobile Application Development

A dedicated mobile application to complement the university’s digital ecosystem, including:

Student scheduling (timetables, classes)

Tracking school fees, payments, and outstanding dues

Online enrollment and admissions

Event creation and event registration system

Notifications and academic updates

B. Advanced University Systems

Student portal with authentication

Staff and faculty portals

Academic records management

Analytics and reporting tools

Role-based access control (students, staff, administration)

Important Note

⚠️ All features listed in this section are not included in the current pricing and timeline.
Their implementation will be planned and quoted separately after the completion of Phase One.

3. Project Evolution & Discussions

Phase One will deliver a fully functional official website within one month.

Future phases will be discussed based on:

University priorities

Budget availability

Growth and operational needs

Giraso commits to preserving scalability, quality, and long-term value throughout all phases.
